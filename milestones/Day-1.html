Week 1
Total: 2 marks
Scope: Team
Deliverable 1
Team
1 mark
Team of 4 members
Choose a sector (e.g. education, health, fintech) that your team wants to focus on.
Describe a market gap or user challenge within that sector, and frame it as a business idea (do not describe a solution yet).
Submit the names and email IDs of all team members along with the chosen sector and problem statement in the provided google sheet. GSheet Link ↗
If teams are not formed by the end of this milestone, the course team will allocate members. Incomplete teams face a 1-mark penalty on this milestone.
Deliverable 2
Team
1 mark
Explore the market and identify apps, products, or services that currently address the same problem.
For each existing solution, describe how it solves the problem and what its shortcomings are. Document these (with links) — they will be included in your GP1 report.
The same solutions identified here cannot be cloned for GP3. Teams will be asked during the GP3 review how they improved upon them. Submitting a cloned solution will be penalised.
