Weeks 7–10
Total: 50 marks
Scope: Team + Individual
Deliverable 1 — User Testing & Validation
Team + Individual
Record 2n videos (min 10 min each) capturing customer experiences with the final prototype.
Submit 2n revised Customer Journey Maps (CJMs) based on testing.
Avoid guiding the customer. Observe and record features they use, avoid, or find difficult. Document all feedback directly into the CJMs.
Deliverable 2 — Canvas 4
Team
Test 1 / Empathize 2 (P2) — Include revised 2n CJMs.
Challenge Identification — Identify new issues introduced by the app from the customer's perspective.
Analyze 2 (P2) & Solve 2 (P2) — Complete the corresponding sections of the canvas.
Post-prototype Iterations — Document final field study iterations and the final prototype.
