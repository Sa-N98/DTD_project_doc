Milestone 3

Test & Deliver Phase Week 7 to Week 10
Submission: Nov 28, 2025
Marks: 50

Students are expected to deliver the following 5 tasks:

Task 1: Test Phase Videos
Record 2n videos (min. 10 min each) of customers testing the final prototype
Each team member conducts at least one test interview
Camera on, audio clear; display name and roll number during testing
Test users must be distinct from GP1 interviewees and from a wide range of backgrounds
Submit 2n revised Customer Journey Maps (CJMs)
Task 2: Canvas 4
Test 1 / Empathise 2 canvas with revised 2n CJMs
Identify new challenges or issues introduced by the app from a customer perspective
Analyse 2 (Phase 2) canvas
Solve 2 (Phase 2) canvas
Post-prototype field study iterations and final prototype on Mural
Task 3: Marketing Video
Create a brief and engaging marketing video between 30 and 60 seconds long
Check spelling, audio quality, and camera clarity before submission
Task 4: Report (2–3 pages)
Describe the KDT-EAST phases followed by the team, specific to your project
Comprehensive list of product/service/app features with assessment of human desirability, business viability, and technological feasibility
Document tools used, accomplishments, lessons learnt, and project closure remarks
Include IITM logo, degree program details, and full names of all team members
Do not include roll numbers, emails, or external links (Figma, Mural, Jira)
Task 5: Presentation & Final App Prototype
Submit final high-fidelity app prototype on a low-code or no-code platform (mandatory)
Record a 5–7 min presentation (includes the 30–60 sec marketing video)
Cover KDT process, wireframe demo/app walkthrough in the presentation
Camera on, audio clear; display name (roll number not required)
