Milestone 1 Details

Welcome to Milestone 1! This milestone focuses on setting up your project and completing the Empathize phase of design thinking. Below are the details for each deliverable, along with resources and important notes to guide you through the process.

Make sure to follow the checklist for each deliverable carefully, and refer to the provided resources for templates and examples. Remember, the quality of your user research and how well you document it will set the foundation for your entire project, so take the time to do it thoroughly.

If you have any questions or need clarification on any of the tasks, please reach out to the course team as soon as possible. Good luck, and we look forward to seeing your progress!
