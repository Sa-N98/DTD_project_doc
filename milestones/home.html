Develop a user-centric strategy using Design Thinking and build an app/website that is user-desirable, business-viable, and technically feasible. Work in teams of four.

Core Requirements
Form a team of 4 by Week 1 and lock in your sector.
Eligibility: Score ≥ 40/50 avg across best 5 of 7 weekly quizzes.
Interview ≥ 4 real target users (team members cannot be users).
Milestone deadlines will NOT be extended.

Final score formula:

F = 0.1 × Quiz + 0.5 × End-term exam + 0.4 × Project
