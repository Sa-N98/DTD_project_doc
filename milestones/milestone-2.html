Deliverable 2 — Bulletproof Your Concept
Team + Individual
5 marks
RD1 — Presenter delivers a 3-minute presentation covering problem statement, Empathize findings, and HMWs to another team. Only the presenter speaks.
RD1 — Dissenters give negative-only feedback for 8 minutes. Presenter listens and records — no refuting.
Team reviews all dissent feedback and decides what to act on. Rework Empathize and Analyze sections as needed. Do not start Solve phase yet.
RD2 — A different member presents updated work (Empathize + Analyze + Solve) to a third team and collects feedback.
Keep camera on and display name and roll number throughout both RD sessions. Audio must be clear.
