Milestone 2

Analyse & Solve Phase Week 4 to Week 6
Submission: Nov 3, 2025
Marks: 25

Students are expected to deliver the following 3 tasks:

Task 1: Analyse Phase — Canvas 2
Pick 2n problems and analyse using the Multi-why technique to explore root causes
Create 2n conflicts of interest and define desired results
Generate 2–3 How Might We (HMW) questions
Create marketing features — app title, icon, category, short description
Update Jira (integrated with Mural) to track your project
Task 2: Bulletproof your concept
Ritual Dissent 1 — present Empathise & Analyse phases to another team, gather feedback
Rework based on feedback (Empathise & Analyse only, not Solve phase yet)
Ritual Dissent 2 — present updated work to a second team, gather feedback
Camera on and audio clear during all dissent sessions; display name and roll number
Submit recorded videos of both Ritual Dissent sessions
Task 3: Canvas 3 — Solve Phase
Generate ideas via silent brainstorming (individual + group parts)
Submit a video/screen recording of individual and group brainstorming session
Develop wireframes of the prototype using Figma or another toolkit
